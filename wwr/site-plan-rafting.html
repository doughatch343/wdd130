Overview

Purpose

Our purpose at Rafting Momentum is to provide adventurers like you the opportunity to take on the thrilling experience of river rafting.

Audience

Question 1: Who are the target customers?

Answer to question 1: Rafting Momentum invites all who wish to take on the challenge of rafting either by yourself, or with friends and family.

Question 2: How Will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?

Answer to question 2: We have created our website to be available to all of our customers, regardless of which device used to access to our website.

Question 3: What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

Answer to question 3: We anticipate our customers looking for information regarding scheduling, prices, group sizes, contact information, and directions.
